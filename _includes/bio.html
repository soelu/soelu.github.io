Bio

A brief history of me.

  • December 1981

    Hello World!

    I was born on December, 4th 1981 in Karl-Marx-Stadt (now Chemnitz) in the former GDR. I grew up as the eldest son of three children in a teacher family.

  • 1999-2010

    First Steps - Excel

    During my late school time and all through my university, I developed a Microsoft Excel based analytics tool for daily group work in classes given by my father. This was my first and a very valuable experience in creating, extending and maintaining a software used in daily work.

  • 2002-2011

    Education is paramount - Dipl.-Inf.

    I decided to get a university degree in computer science at the Technische Universit√§t Dresden and used that time to dive into the broad spectrum of computer science and related topics. Out of curiosity I took Psychology and Business English as minors, courses of which I benefit until today. I finish my studies in November 2011 with a Diploma in Computer Science (Informatik).

  • 2005-2012

    The first project - Projekt StEG

    Serendipitously, a friend of mine and I got the opportunity to develop a PHP based platform used for giving feedback to participants of a research study for the German Institute for International Educational Research (DIPF). We created, extended and maintained the platform in three phases and successfully delivered all the features needed.
    Without knowing we already used so to say agile methods to test different approaches. Already then, I felt comfortable to do customer communication and negotiations.

  • 2012-2015

    Kiwigrid - The Early Years

    I started at Kiwigrid as a PHP backend developer. Soon I gained more and more responsibility in this young start-up. I quickly rose to the role of team lead and software architect. The first really customer driven project at Kiwigrid was a Home Energy Management Solution for SOLARWATT. It was technically lead by myself and consisted most of the time of a team of four developers.

  • 2015-2017

    Kiwigrid - The Later Years

    In 2015 Kiwigrid won a project focused on intelligent charging of electric vehicles by BMW. I lead the team in the role of the Product Owner and part time ScrumMaster. The solution was delivered in time and budget and stands out in my experience as large-scale product development embedded in a large co-operation.

  • 07/2017-11/2017

    DevBoost - Something New

    In July 2017 I joined DevBoost as a Product Manager to build a mobile ticketing platform for general entrance tickets like zoos, museeums or amusement parks. It was a new and exhilarating idea that I happily helped to shape. This also entailed a lot of sales activity to excite potential partners for the idea to join the ticket marketplace twickly.
    The endevour is still ongoing and we will see what lies ahead.

  • 12/2017 - 12/2019

    Kiwigrid - Recoding Energy

    After a couple of exiting months at DevBoost, the world of energy management and renewable energy still wouldn't leave my thoughts. In December 2017 I decided to rejoin Kiwigrid. This decision was only made possible after some important and fundamental changes where implemented at Kiwigrid. I am confident that this new path will lead Kiwigrid to great success.

  • 01/2020

    Staffbase - People First!

    Staffbase is the leading mobile employee relationship platform for large or decentralized organizations. As a learned in my previous engagements that communication is key for well running company, I decided to join Staffbase to help companies to put people first. I started as a Senior Project Manager for Onboarding new customers. After only 3 months a already worked in onboarding projects with six or more customers in parallel.
